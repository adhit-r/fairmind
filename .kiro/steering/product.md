# FairMind Product Overview

FairMind is a comprehensive ethical AI sandbox and governance platform that provides advanced bias detection, explainability, and compliance capabilities for modern AI systems, particularly focusing on generative AI and LLMs.

## Core Purpose
- **AI Governance**: Complete lifecycle management for AI models with bias detection and fairness analysis
- **Modern Bias Detection**: Latest 2025 research methods including WEAT, SEAT, Minimal Pairs, and Red Teaming for LLMs
- **Multimodal Analysis**: Cross-modal bias detection for Image, Audio, Video, and Text generation
- **Explainability Integration**: Integration with CometLLM, DeepEval, Arize Phoenix, and AWS Clarify
- **Compliance & Security**: OWASP AI security testing, regulatory compliance tracking, and AI Bill of Materials

## Key Features
- **Traditional AI Governance**: 8 core features including bias detection, AI DNA profiling, model registry
- **Modern LLM Bias Detection**: 4 new features with cutting-edge bias detection methods
- **Real-time Monitoring**: Live bias detection, alerting, and compliance reporting
- **Production Ready**: 45+ API endpoints with comprehensive testing and deployment automation

## Target Users
- AI/ML Engineers implementing responsible AI practices
- Data Scientists requiring bias detection and fairness analysis
- Compliance teams managing AI governance and regulatory requirements
- Organizations deploying AI systems requiring ethical oversight

## Business Value
- Reduces AI bias and improves model fairness
- Ensures regulatory compliance (GDPR, AI Act, CCPA)
- Provides comprehensive audit trails and documentation
- Enables responsible AI deployment at scale
